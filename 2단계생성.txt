## 📷 카메라 앵글 필수 규칙



### 구조별 권장 앵글

| 씬 구조 | 권장 앵글 | 프롬프트 키워드 |

|--------|----------|---------------|

| HOOK (1) | **close-up** | close-up portrait shot, face in focus, shallow depth of field |

| SETUP (2~3) | **wide** | wide establishing shot, full body visible, environment context, deep focus |

| BUILD-UP (4~6) | **wide / OTS / POV / aerial** | wide shot, over-the-shoulder, first-person POV, aerial shot |

| CLIMAX (7~8) | **close-up / medium** | close-up shot, dramatic expression, face in focus, or medium reaction |

| TWIST (9~10) | **wide / aerial / high-angle** | wide shot revealing context, aerial view, high-angle shot |

| OUTRO (11~12) | **medium / wide** | medium shot, relaxed framing, or wide establishing |



### 앵글 다양성 체크리스트

- ✅ close-up **최대 2개**

- ✅ wide **최소 4개**

- ✅ medium **1~2개**

- ✅ over-the-shoulder 또는 POV **최소 1개**

- ✅ aerial/high-angle **최소 1개**

- ❌ 같은 앵글 2연속 금지

## 🎥 POV (1인칭 시점) 샷 절대 규칙 ⚠️
